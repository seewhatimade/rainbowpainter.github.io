# Rainbow Painter

A free drawing canvas with brushes, stamps, sparkles, and rainbow colors!

## How to Play

1. **Pick a tool** from the toolbar: Brush, Sparkle Spray, Eraser, or Fill Bucket.
2. **Choose a color** from the palette, or turn on **Rainbow mode** to cycle through colors automatically.
3. **Select a brush size**: S, M, L, or XL.
4. **Draw** on the canvas by moving your mouse or finger.
5. Add fun **emoji stamps** -- stars, hearts, animals, and more (18 total).
6. Hit **Save** to download your masterpiece as a PNG image.
7. Hit **New** to start a fresh canvas.

## Controls

- **Mouse / Touch** -- draw on the canvas
- **Toolbar buttons** -- switch tools, colors, and sizes

## Details

- Custom paintbrush cursor shows your current color and size
- 18 emoji stamps to decorate your art
- 4 brush sizes and a full color palette
- Rainbow mode auto-cycles through every hue as you draw
